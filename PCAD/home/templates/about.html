About Us

Who We Are

PremiumCAD has an extensive experience in CAD / GIS industry of India and has been provider of quality manpower and contractual delivery of complex projects to various outsourcing companies in India catering to a host of industries across the globe.
We have a decade-plus of involvement and experience in designing residential solar plans. We provide a wide range of services in this division ranging from sales to interconnection. Our team has an in-depth understanding of AHJ and Utility requirements and are familiar with various codes and compliances.
We started our journey as freelancer offering Solar PV Design Services and Residential Solar permit plans for installers in US. We helped leading Indian companies to set up their outsourcing operations. We have experience designing in residential solar plans.
Now PremiumCAD has a team of over 145 employees with dedicated training centers and tie-up with some of the technical institutes and colleges in the NCR of India. We Build Operate and Transfer projects which are later white labeled and delivered to Global Clientele of our Indian Clients.
After years of experience and augmenting necessary office infrastructure, hardware and dedicated sales and marketing potential, PremiumCAD is ready to offer the services directly to the clients.


What We Do

We are the preferred outsourcing partner for CAD & GIS based Engineering designs. With an experienced and a highly motivated team of professionals, we provide a competitive edge to our clients in a price-sensitive market by offering scalable and timely deliveries with agreed quality SLAs.
