From Site Assessment to Building Permit: A Guide to Solar Permit Design

PremiumCAD • 24 January • 2 min read

Solar permit design is an important aspect of the solar panel installation process. It involves the preparation of detailed drawings and documentation that are required to obtain a building permit for the installation of a solar power system. The solar permit design process can vary depending on local building codes and regulations, but generally includes the following steps:

  • Site assessment: A professional will visit the property to determine the best location for solar panels and the size and type of system that would be most efficient. Factors such as the orientation of the property, the amount of shading, and local weather conditions are taken into consideration.
  • System design: Based on the site assessment, a detailed plan for the solar power system is created. This includes the number and type of solar panels, the location of the panels, and any necessary equipment such as inverters and batteries. The design must also comply with local building codes and regulations.
  • Drawings and documentation: Detailed drawings of the proposed solar power system, including electrical schematics, are prepared. These drawings, along with other required documentation, such as calculations and specifications, are used to apply for the building permit.
  • Building permit application: The permit application is submitted to the local building department. The application will be reviewed by the building official to ensure that the proposed solar power system meets all local building codes and regulations.
  • Inspection and approval: Once the building permit is approved, the installation of the solar power system can begin. The installation will be inspected by the building department to ensure that it complies with the approved design and building codes. Once the installation is complete, the final inspection will be conducted, and the building permit will be closed.

Conclusion

Obtaining a solar permit is a crucial step in the solar panel installation process as it ensures that the installation complies with local building codes and regulations and that the solar power system is safe and efficient. Hiring a professional that has experience in solar permit design can save time and ensure that the permit application is complete and accurate.
