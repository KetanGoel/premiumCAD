How to Choose the Right Solar PV Design Company

PremiumCAD • 7 April • 2 min read

Solar power is a great way to save money on your energy bills and reduce your carbon footprint. However, with so many solar PV design companies to choose from, it can be tough to know which one is right for you.

Here are a few tips to help you choose the right solar PV design company:

  • Get multiple quotes. Don't just go with the first solar PV design company you find. Get quotes from at least three different companies to compare prices and services.
  • Check the company's credentials. Make sure the company you choose is licensed, bonded, and insured. You should also check to see if they have any accreditations, such as the Solar Power Industries Association (SPIA) or the North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners (NABCEP).
  • Ask about the company's experience. How long have they been in business? How many solar PV systems have they installed? The more experience a company has, the more likely they are to do a good job.
  • Talk to past customers. Ask the solar PV design company for references from past customers. This will give you a chance to hear about their experiences with the company and the quality of their work.
  • Get everything in writing. Once you've chosen a solar PV design company, make sure you get everything in writing. This includes the price of the system, the terms of the warranty, and the timeline for installation.

By following these tips, you can be sure to choose the right solar PV design company for your needs.


Additional Considerations

In addition to the factors listed above, there are a few other things to consider when choosing a solar PV design company.

  • The company's financing options. Solar can be a big upfront investment, so it's important to find a company that offers financing options.
  • The company's customer service. You want to be sure that you can count on the company to answer your questions and address any problems that may arise after your system is installed.
  • The company's commitment to sustainability. Do you want to work with a company that is committed to sustainability? Some solar PV design companies are more committed to sustainability than others.


By considering all of these factors, you can be sure to choose the right solar PV design company for your needs.
